About Us

A Sugar House-based python web developer meetup. We are interested in promoting python development, but also welcome wisdom found in other programming languages. Avid and aspiring programmers alike are welcome to join us.

We alternate our meeting between the first Wednesday and the first Thursday of the month at Zaniac Sugar House (the old Sugar House coffee location; the big purple building).

For current news follow us using Meetup.com
